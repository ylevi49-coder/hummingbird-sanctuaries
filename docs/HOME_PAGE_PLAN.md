HOME PAGE PLAN

The homepage is the emotional and visual centerpiece of the Hummingbird Sanctuaries brand.

Its purpose is to:

create emotional connection
establish trust
communicate mission
create donor confidence
present a world-class nonprofit image
guide users into deeper engagement
increase donations and long-term support

The homepage must feel:

cinematic
emotionally powerful
elegant
spacious
trustworthy
premium
human-centered

The homepage should never feel:

generic
corporate
templated
crowded
overly salesy
SECTION 1 — HERO SECTION
Purpose

Create an immediate emotional connection and establish visual identity.

Emotional Goal

The visitor should feel:

hope
calm
emotional curiosity
trust
inspiration
Visual Direction
full-screen cinematic hero
emotional image or video
dark gradient overlay
elegant serif headline
gold CTA button
subtle motion
Content Direction

Headline should be short, cinematic, emotional.

Subheadline should:

explain mission simply
feel human
avoid generic nonprofit language
Assets Needed
cinematic hero image or video
natural lighting
emotional realism
sanctuary atmosphere
CTA

Primary:

Donate Now
Support Healing
Create Sanctuary

Secondary:

Learn Our Mission
Watch Our Story
