ASSET PLAN

This document defines the visual asset direction for the Hummingbird Sanctuaries website.

The visual assets are one of the most important parts of the brand experience.

All imagery and video must support the cinematic humanitarian luxury direction.

The website should visually feel:

emotional
cinematic
warm
premium
trustworthy
human
elegant
authentic

Never generic.

MASTER VISUAL DIRECTION

The overall visual identity should feel like a combination of:

luxury humanitarian foundation
documentary storytelling
boutique sanctuary environments
emotional realism
modern premium nonprofit

Inspirations:

Apple emotional campaigns
Aman Resorts
Netflix documentary cinematography
high-end humanitarian storytelling
